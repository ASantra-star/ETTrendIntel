
# ET TradeIntel — Supply Chain × Market Intelligence

## Overview

ET TradeIntel is an AI-powered platform that integrates supply chain monitoring with financial market intelligence to generate real-time, actionable insights.

It connects disruptions in logistics and production with stock market behavior, enabling smarter operational and investment decisions.

---

## Features

* Nexus Intelligence Engine (Supply × Market correlation)
* Real-time disruption tracking
* AI-driven stock signals
* Risk analysis and SLA monitoring
* Supplier intelligence dashboard
* Interactive charts and analytics
* AI chat assistant for insights

---

## Architecture

Frontend:

* HTML, CSS, JavaScript
* Chart.js for visualization

AI Layer:

* LLM-based inference (Groq / LLaMA)

Modules:

* Nexus Intelligence
* Market Signals
* Supply Chain Monitoring
* Risk Matrix
* Supplier Intelligence

---

## How AI is Used

* Correlates supply chain disruptions with stock movements
* Generates predictive insights and trading signals
* Calculates risk and confidence scores
* Suggests mitigation actions (rerouting, sourcing)
* Provides conversational insights via chat interface

---

## Use Cases

* Traders: Identify early stock signals
* Supply Chain Teams: Monitor and respond to disruptions
* Enterprises: Manage operational and financial risks

---

## Getting Started

1. Open `tradeintel.html` in a browser
2. Navigate through modules using the sidebar
3. Explore:

   * Nexus Intelligence for insights
   * Market Signals for trading data
   * Supply Chain for disruptions
4. Use the AI chat panel for queries
